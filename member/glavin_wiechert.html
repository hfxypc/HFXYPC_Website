New beginnings!

In the year 2006, something big happened: my family got an iMac! My dad finally got fed up with his Windows PCs and took the leap into Mac. As soon as I got home my brother and I had taken over and were exploring all the new found posibilites that we could do with a computer: Garage Band was our favourite, at the time. After a while my brother strayed from our curious playing and my dad realized that Mac OS X Tiger (Operating System for Macs at the time), acts very differently than Windows. He gave up and went back to his frustratingly misbehaved Windows computer, and left me with the iMac.  I was thrilled! From then on, things only got better. 

I eventually found out that I could actually write my own programs and applications and dove into AppleScript with enthusiasm, starting small and working my way up towards more complex programs in baby steps. This is where my thirst for computer science and programming emanated.
